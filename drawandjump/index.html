ENEMY GUIDE

Spikes Placer

Moves side to side along platforms, dropping deadly spikes behind. Stay above to avoid its trail of destruction!

Snake

Slithers along platforms and changes direction at edges. Quick and agile, this enemy is hard to avoid on narrow platforms!

Big Boi

Stationary but dangerous! Shoots projectiles when you get too close. Keep your distance or create platforms to shield yourself!

Ghost

Phases in and out of visibility, making it hard to avoid. Can float through the air in a figure-8 pattern and pass through platforms!

Flower Dragon

Transforms between a harmless flower and a fire-breathing dragon! When open, it shoots fireballs in random directions. Watch out!

HUGE BOI

The mighty boss! Has three attack phases and can only be damaged when vulnerable (pink). Jump on its head when it's vulnerable to deal damage!
